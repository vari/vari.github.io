About

I am a 1B software engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I would describe myself as an enthusiastic programmer who loves coding and solving problems. My favourite part of coding is being able to order a computer around and tell it what to do. I love finding new ways of doing things and coming up with clever solutions to the challenges that arise while completing projects.

Debugging is another aspect of coding I enjoy. Why? Let's face it, the most tedious and sometimes extremely stressful part of coding is debugging, and yet it can be the most enjoyable. When things don't go as planned, and there are weird bugs, that's when I can put my problem solving skills to the test. And, not to mention, the exhilaration having fully functional code without bugs and sense of completeness after debugging is without parallel.
